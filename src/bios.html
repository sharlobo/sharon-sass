Meet the Band!


Harvey Dale

Harvey Dale is the electrifying lead guitarist and vocalist of the world-renowned band, "Harvey Dale and the Cements" With his guitar solos and powerful stage presence, Harvey Dale has become an iconic figure in the music industry.


Ziggy Nightshade


Ziggy Nightshade is the heartbeat and driving force behind the explosive sound of "Harvey Dale and the Cements" one of the most iconic rock bands of our time. With his relentless rhythm and thunderous beats, Ziggy is the powerhouse that keeps the band's music alive and kicking.


Roxy Rebelson

Roxy Rebelson is the undeniable heartbeat of "Harvey Dale and the Cements." With her thumping bass lines and magnetic stage presence, Roxy is the embodiment of rock 'n' roll.
